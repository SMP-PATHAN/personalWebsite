Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age limit to join?

We welcome students of all ages, starting from 5 years old.

What are the class timings?

Classes are available on weekdays from 4 PM to 7 PM and weekends from 10 AM to 2 PM.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer both online and offline classes.

What is the fee structure?

Our fee structure varies depending on the course and level. Please contact us for detailed information.

What equipment do I need for online classes?

For online classes, you will need a computer or tablet with internet access and a chess board for practice.

Do you provide training for chess tournaments?

Yes, we provide specialized training for students preparing for chess tournaments.

Can I get a trial class?

Yes, we offer one free trial class to new students.

How can I register?

You can register online through our website or visit our academy in person.

About Us

Vision

Our vision is to nurture young minds to develop strategic thinking and problem-solving skills through the game of chess.

Mission

Our mission is to provide quality chess education and training to enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels, fostering a love for the game and promoting intellectual growth.
